Biography

Dr. Chandranshu Jagtap is a dedicated and compassionate dental professional, a graduate of the government dental college Indore, with a strong commitment to quality dental care and patient well-being. He has previously been associated with the Government Dental College and MY Hospital, Indore, and has also served as an Ex-Dental Surgeon at the Community Health Center, Rajpur, Barwani (M.P.).

Dr. Jagtap has attended several advanced dental and aesthetic conferences and strongly believes that effective dentistry lies in the conservation and restoration of natural teeth. With a special focus on cosmetic dentistry and endodontics, he ensures precise, ethical, and patient-centered care in a safe and friendly environment.
