Reliable & High-Quality Service - Jagtap dental clinc

At Jagtap Dental Clinic, we provide reliable, high-quality dental care with a strong focus on comfort, safety, and hygiene. Every visit is aimed at giving you a healthy smile you can trust.

Dental Examinations

Regular dental check-ups to assess oral health, identify potential issues, and plan appropriate treatments.

Root Canal Therapy

Treatment to remove infected pulp from inside the tooth, relieving pain and saving the tooth.

Tooth Extractions

Removal of damaged or impacted teeth with minimal discomfort.

X-Rays

Digital imaging to provide a detailed view of your teeth and jaw, aiding in accurate diagnosis.

Orthodontics

Solutions for straightening teeth and correcting bite issues, including Invisalign and traditional braces.

Dentures

Removable appliances to replace multiple missing teeth, restoring function and appearance.

Partial Dentures

Removable partial dentures designed to blend seamlessly with natural teeth.

Crowns

Caps placed over damaged teeth to restore their shape, size, and function.

Oral Cancer Screenings

Early detection and assessment of oral cancer through thorough examinations.

Smile Design

Tailored cosmetic dental plans to achieve your desired smile aesthetics.

All Paediatric Dental Care

Care for your child's smile with gentle pediatric dentistry, including check-ups, preventive care, and treatments for happy teeth.

Full Mouth Reconstruction

Extensive treatment plans to restore the health, function, and appearance of your entire mouth.

Cracked Teeth Treatment

Specialized care to diagnose and treat cracked teeth, preventing further damage and restoring tooth structure and function.

Traumatic Dental Injury Care

Immediate treatment for teeth damaged by trauma, helping to save and restore injured teeth.

Pulp Capping and Regeneration

Procedures to preserve the dental pulp's vitality and promote natural healing and regeneration, especially in young or developing teeth.

Crowns and Bridges

Custom-made to restore strength, function, and appearance to damaged or missing teeth, closely matching your natural teeth.

Veneers

Thin porcelain shells that correct imperfections, enhancing the front surface of your teeth for a perfect smile.

Wisdom Teeth Extraction

Safe and effective removal of impacted or problematic wisdom teeth to prevent complications and promote oral health.

Facial Trauma Treatment

Comprehensive care for injuries to the face, mouth, and jaws, ensuring proper healing and restoration of function and aesthetics.

Corrective Jaw Surgery

Surgical procedures to correct jaw misalignment, improving function, appearance, and overall oral health.

Dental Implants

Surgical placement of dental implants to replace missing teeth, providing a stable and long-lasting solution.
